About Zettabit Ltd

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Zettabit Ltd has established itself as a specialized provider of Oracle PeopleSoft systems administration and IT services in New Zealand. We are a small, focused team based in Auckland with a deep understanding of enterprise software systems.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, expert remote and onsite administration services for Oracle PeopleSoft systems, enabling organizations to focus on their core business while we manage their critical IT infrastructure.

Our Expertise

With years of experience in PeopleSoft administration, we specialize in:

  • Oracle PeopleSoft system administration and support
  • Remote technical expertise and troubleshooting
  • Onsite implementation and configuration
  • Enterprise software consulting and strategy
  • Managed IT services and support

Our Partnership

We work in partnership with CXC Global, a leading provider of remote IT expertise and services. This partnership allows us to:

  • Access a broader network of specialized expertise
  • Provide seamless remote support to our clients
  • Simplify invoicing and payroll processes
  • Scale our services according to client needs

Why Choose Zettabit?

  • Specialized Knowledge: Deep expertise in Oracle PeopleSoft systems
  • Flexible Support: Both remote and onsite support options
  • Reliable Service: Trusted by organizations across New Zealand
  • Professional Team: Experienced, qualified IT professionals
  • Responsive: Quick response times and dedicated support
